## Concept Structure

### Three Pillars
1. **Sips** — Tailor-made cocktails and premium beverages
2. **Bites** — Comfort food with a twist
3. **Sounds** — Curated music that defines the atmosphere

### Menu Philosophy (Construction Metaphor)
- Food = "Built, not served"
- Categories = Stages of construction
- Plates = Structural elements
- "Form follows flavor"
- "Nothing is random. Everything has alignment."

### Naming System
- **Cocktails** → Architects (Le Corbusier, Renzo Piano, Ziller)
- **Smoothies** → Museums (MOMA, Munch, Mori, Guggenheim, Pompidou)
- **Shots** → Compounds
- **Food items** → Structural codes (System A, Structure 01, FL-01, etc.)

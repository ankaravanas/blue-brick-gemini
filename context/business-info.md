# Business Info — Blue Brick

## Overview

**Blue Brick** is a new all-day hospitality venue located on **Οδός Δραγατσάνιου**, in the heart of Athens. It opened (or is opening) in **March 2026** and has already generated significant pre-launch buzz across Athenian social media and lifestyle press.

## Concept

Blue Brick positions itself as an **authentic Hybrid Concept** — not a café, not a bar, not a restaurant, but a living organism that transforms throughout the day. The three pillars of the concept are:

- **Sips** — Tailor-made cocktails and premium beverage blends
- **Bites** — Comfort food with a twist, designed for every moment of the day
- **Sounds** — Curated music that defines the atmosphere, never background noise

The venue's tagline is **"Break the Brick"** — a call to break the stereotypes of conventional entertainment and hospitality.

## Location

- **Address:** Οδός Δραγατσάνιου, Athens city center
- **Area character:** Δραγατσάνιου has become the city's premier trend-setting street, driven in part by the massive success of DR8. Blue Brick aims to be the next major chapter on this street.
- **Positioning:** Urban core, premium-casual, culturally relevant

## Brand Identity

- **Name:** Blue Brick
- **Tagline:** Break the Brick
- **Tone:** Bold, curated, anti-stereotype, intentional
- **Aesthetic:** Contemporary, hybrid, premium yet accessible
- **Vibe:** All-day, living space that evolves — morning coffee culture into evening cocktail culture

## Target Audience

- Athenians aged 25–40 who follow trends and seek curated experiences
- Visitors and tourists drawn to Athens' emerging cultural hotspots
- F&B enthusiasts who appreciate quality over quantity
- People who spend time in the Σύνταγμα / Μοναστηράκι / Δραγατσάνιου corridor

## Social Media Presence

- **Instagram:** @blue__brick__ (active pre-launch, already viral)
- The venue executed a strategic pre-launch social media campaign that generated anticipation before the doors even opened — indicating a marketing-first mindset behind the project

## Competitive Context

- Direct neighbor/predecessor: **DR8** (which transformed Δραγατσάνιου into a trend-setting street)
- General competitive set: All-day premium bars and hybrid concepts in central Athens
- Differentiation: Blue Brick is not just another all-day spot — it is a deliberately crafted experience with a distinct concept, strong branding, and a clear editorial voice across sips, bites, and sounds
